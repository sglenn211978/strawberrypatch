Our Story

Why Fresh Eggs
Actually Matter

That carton of "Grade A Large" eggs sitting on the grocery shelf? It may be up to 60 days old. Legally fine. But legal isn't the same as what you actually want for your family's breakfast.

At Strawberry Patch Eggs, our eggs go from nest to your hands within 24–48 hours. Deeper orange yolks. Firmer whites. Flavor that turns a Tuesday morning into something worth waking up for.

We started this because we wanted to know exactly what was going into our food — and into our kids' food. Our birds eat a non-GMO diet, forage freely on pasture, and are never given growth hormones or preventive antibiotics. Happy birds. Better eggs. Simple as that.

"We don't just sell eggs. We sell the alternative to not knowing where your food comes from."
